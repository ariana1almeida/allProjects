We apologize for any inconvenience this has caused anyone.

All purchased tickets will remain valid.

Tickets initially bought for 06 July 2020, 06 July 2021, 21 June 2022 will allow entry for the show on 17 June 2022.

Tickets initially bought for 07 July 2020, 07 July 2021, 22 June 2022 will allow entry for the show on 18 June 2022.
